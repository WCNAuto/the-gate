This is not a productivity tool. It is a coherence tool.

Most costly decisions, in business, in money, in marriage, in private appetites, follow the same engine. Instinct first. System second. By the time the system kicks in, the cost has already landed.

The gate runs the other way. You post the impulse before you act on it. The mirror runs it against your stated contract and your known patterns and gives you the honest read. Not permission. Not refusal. The actual call.

Use it for decisions that would normally bypass your better judgment. Investments. Hires. Conversations you are avoiding. Desires you are rationalising. Not for trivial choices.

The gate is only as honest as the contract. Edit the contract to be true. Update it when the man you are evolves. Run the gate before the appetite runs you.
